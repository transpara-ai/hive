Read the full history in loop/reflections.md.
Read loop/state.md.

COVER: Did this iteration explore the right territory? Did the Scout look at the right things? Was anything relevant ignored?

BLIND: What are we NOT seeing? What assumptions might be wrong? What parts of the system haven't been touched in many iterations? Flag uncertainty honestly. FIXPOINT CHECK: If the Scout found no gaps or recent iterations all feel complete, that's a local fixpoint, not proof of global completeness. Invoke Blind hardest when everything feels done.

ZOOM: Are we at the right scale? Too granular? Too ambitious? Should the next iteration zoom in or out?

FORMALIZE: Look at recent iterations as a body of work. Are patterns emerging? Name the current cluster. Is it complete?

Append to loop/reflections.md:
- Iteration number and date
- What was built (one line)
- COVER / BLIND / ZOOM assessments
- FORMALIZE: method improvements if needed
- One sentence: what the Scout should focus on next

Update loop/state.md:
- Current system state (accurate, from code)
- Lessons learned (cumulative)
- Completed clusters
- Known issues
- What the Scout should focus on next

Close the iteration: `./loop/close.sh` — this commits loop/ artifacts to the current feature branch. It reads loop/config.env for git remote and org settings. It does NOT push or post unless configured to do so. Review the commit before proceeding.
